Often, we procrastinate for somewhat time-consuming tasks. Watching long videos is definitely one of them. Have you ever added a TED video to your watch-later list only to forget about it later on? No worries, we are he-re to present to you the shortened versions of some really fascinating TEDx talks we love. Bytes are the highli-ghts of our favourite TEDx talks from both within our organisation and others across the world.

Episode 1 – Detecting Sarcasm, Combating Homophobia.

Hey Siri! Tell me something computationally sarcastic!
Here’s our favourite – "Humans and computers are similar in many ways, just that computers are not known to be homophobic" ~ Aditya Joshi
Watch this super cool talk on teaching sarcasm to robots.

Episode 2 – How Storytelling saved me

The Arabian Nights. Twinkling lamps. Aladdin with his genie and the beautiful World of Stories. Stories speak of the wonderful connection we have with our world. The healing factor what Stories possess is said to do wonders in one's life! Watch Mehek Mirza Prabhu as she narrates the tale of how her "Aladdin" navigated her through a difficult and mysterious journey and how she came victorious in the end!

Episode 3 – Like a Sufi

The journey of life is difficult to walk. What we make of this journey is on us. Watch Alif Band as they sing and share their way of life with the rest of us in this TEDx Byte!

Episode 4 - Fighting OCD

Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder : Noun, abbreviation: OCD Psychiatry: An anxiety disorder in which patients are driven to repeat the same act, such as washing their hands, over and over again, usually for many hours. Watch Jay Thadeshwar talk about fighting OCD in this amazing TED talk about his struggle with it and how he overcame it to say: “I have OCD but OCD does not have me”.

Episode 5 – Why We Play Games

"Life is a game" has been a fundamental teaching of countless spiritual leaders, philosophers, musicians, and poets. What do you like most about playing games? For Jon, it's the spirit of the game. Check out this TEDx Talk Byte where Jon Dallas explains what it might look like for us to begin playing our lives as a game.

Episode 6 - Run with a soldier, run for a soldier

“The Duty” as a soldier is to protect the nation from our enemies but as a citizen of this nation what’s our duty? Watch this TEDx Talk Byte, Sundaresan Renganathan makes us realise how we can help the families of the martyrs of our nation and truly accomplish our duty as a citizen.

Episode 7 - Why Your Stories Matter

"A story gets you from within" each and every story deserves to be shared, deserves to be read, deserves to be heard, who is anyone to decide what story is worth sharing and what story isn't? Watch this TEDx Talk Byte, Rabia Kapoor talks about the importance of sharing your story to change lives be it yours or anyone else's.
